Lasagna alla bolognese

Description

This is the recipe of lasagna alla bolognese

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • 1 or 2 onions
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 800g tomato sauce
  • 500g grounded beef
  • Oregano, pepper
  • Mozzarella
  • Gruyere cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven at 200 degrees C
  2. If the pasta is not already cooked, cook it according to box instructions
  3. In a pan, cook the onion,ground beef and garlic
  4. Add the tomato sauce, oregano, pepper and salt
  5. Butter the oven plate
  6. At the bottom add a layer of pasta, then bolognese sauce and then gruyere
  7. Repeat precedent steps til no more and finally add the mozzarella

This is the recipe of lasagna alla bolognese

Ingredients

  • 120g of brown sugar
  • 120g of white sugar
  • 175g of butter
  • 75g of egg (~1 egg)
  • 300g of flour (T45)
  • 6g of baking powder (1 bag)
  • Half a chocolate bar (at your desire)

Steps

  1. Cut the butter in little pieces
  2. Mix the sugars, flour and baking powder
  3. Add the chocolate
  4. Roll the dough to make big sausages and wrap it up
  5. Put it in the fridge at least half an hour
  6. Preheat the oven at 170Â° C
  7. Cut slices of 2,5 cm of the dough sausage and put the slices on a baking sheet
  8. Cook them from 5 to 10 minutes (they should be a little browner)
